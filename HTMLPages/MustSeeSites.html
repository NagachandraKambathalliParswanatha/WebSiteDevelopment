Incredible entertainment, historic architecture, glorious food, vibrant culture and a night life that never stops: Montréal has it all, but where to start? Ask any local about where to find the best espresso or most authentic poutine and you'll get 10 different answers, but when it comes to the fundamentals of getting to know Montréal, several attractions and activities stand out. From the fresh air and city views of Mount Royal to late-night carousing, put these on your to-do-in-Montréal list.

Montréal Botanical Garden

One of the tallest and most recognizable architectural structures in the city, the leaning Montréal Tower, built for the 1976 summer Olympics, gives visitors a 360-degree view of the city from its observation deck. Return to earth to visit the many worlds of the Space for Life: the impressive greenhouse and sprawling grounds of the Montréal Botanical Garden; the life-filled oceans, tropics, forests and antarctic shores of the Biodôme; the critters of the Insectarium; and galactic adventures of the Planetarium.

Old Montréal streets

Want a piece of Montréal to go? Go shopping! Find Quebec designers galore on the cobblestone streets of Old Montréal. Montréal is a city rich with style, and the quickest way to get a taste of it is by perusing our many, many boutiques. Whether it’s narrow Old Montréal streets studded with designer lairs or big shopping districts where you’ll find all the popular names in retail, it’s here. Take a day to explore our eclectic antique shops, elegant art galleries, pop-up shops, open-air markets, tiny craft shops or large department stores – there are great buys for every taste, budget and need. Just keep in mind: in Montréal, we set rather than follow trends.

Light Life

Listen to local bands and sounds from around the world at live music venues. Dance the tango or make your own moves to late-night electro. And from summer to winter, join the jovial festival crowds outdoors for live music and cultural performances, great food and drink.Even with so much to do after dark, Montréal's always experimenting with new drinks, DJs and late-night festival happenings. From tried-and-true sports bars and chilled-out patios to the latest indie rock bands, the night belongs to everyone.

Mount Royal

Mount Royal is in backyard of Montréal. This gentle giant offers a relaxing escape for nature lovers, sports enthusiasts, families and anyone who just wants to take a breather. Go all the way to the top for stunning views of the city and countryside.Climb the 256-step staircase to the top for a cardio workout, followed by captivating views.Train your eyes on the trees or watch the feeders: the mountain is home to an array of bird species.Spring, summer, fall and winter, Mount Royal offers you an array of activities and a great escape from the city’s hustle and bustle.
